INTRODUCING THE FIRST EVER CULTURAL NIGHT..!

Greetings Prospective Delegates ,

To add a soothing breeze to the furious and intensifying debates and the discussions that you shall in witness in the committee sessions, BVBARRS MUN gives you a rejuvenating and enthralling CULTURAL NIGHT. The Bhavan’s cultural evening featuring energetic dance troupes, vibrant musical performances and tasty delicacies from different regions will turn the school’s auditorium into a multi-cultural showcase from 6:00-7:30 pm, Saturday, July 16. This program will highlight our immense cultural diversity! We look forward to seeing all of the familiar and new faces in the audience!
